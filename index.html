Phenomenal Pomodoro App

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s.
The technique uses a timer to break down work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks.

Use Spacebar to start/pause, up/down arrows to adjust session time, and left/right arrow to adjust break time.

After a session completes, bells will ring. After a break completes, a piano stacatto will play.
